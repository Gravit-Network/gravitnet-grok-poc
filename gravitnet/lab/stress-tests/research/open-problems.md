# Open Research Problems in Gravit Trust

This document lists the most important unsolved or partially solved questions.

---

## High Priority

1. How to formally prove convergence of trust propagation under adaptive multi-phase adversaries?
2. What is the optimal memory window and μ for historical stability?
3. How to make reputation tiers dynamically adjustable without governance capture?
4. Can RL adversaries learn to evade phase-aware anomaly detection?
5. How to implement reputation irreversibility in a fully decentralized way?

---

## Medium Priority

6. Cross-network trust interoperability (different protocols)
7. Zero-knowledge trust attestations without revealing history
8. Multi-agent adversarial coordination (group RL)
9. Phase-aware defense adaptation (system learns to counter)
10. Long-term drift in trust memory under noisy signals

---

## How to Contribute

• Add new problems with GCP reference
• Propose experiments or simulations
• Submit paper drafts or notebooks

All open problems are tracked here and discussed via GCP (Informational track).
